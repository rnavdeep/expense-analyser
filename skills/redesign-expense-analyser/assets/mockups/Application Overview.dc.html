Capabilities

Everything needed to capture, organise and settle expenses

Built as a focused toolkit around a single job: removing the manual work from shared expense tracking.

Automatic receipt reading

Upload images or PDFs and AWS Textract extracts merchant, date, tax, totals and individual line items — no typing.

Organised expense list

Create, edit and delete expenses with powerful filtering, multi-field search, sorting and pagination across large histories.

Share with friends

Add friends by email and assign them to expenses to split costs, then track everyone's shared contributions in one view.

Real-time notifications

A SignalR hub pushes instant updates when a document finishes processing or an expense is shared with you.

Document results view

Every processed receipt opens to a structured breakdown of extracted line items and computed totals.

Secure sessions

Cookie-based authentication with automatic session validation and protected routes guarding every private view.
